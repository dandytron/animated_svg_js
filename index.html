Reuters SVG Animation Tool beta

Animate a Datawrapper chart and export it for video.

  1. Load a chart — paste a Datawrapper ID or URL, open an SVG file, or click Test to try a sample. Works best on bar charts and line graphs.
  2. Build the animation — click chart elements (or Queue all) and tweak the timings. Click titles or footnotes to hide them.
  3. Preview and export — GIF, animated SVG, or transparent ProRes video for Premiere.

You can also paste SVG markup anywhere on this page.
